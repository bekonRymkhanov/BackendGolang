About Us

Welcome to BookRight, your trusted companion in discovering your next favorite book. Founded in 2024, BookRight leverages advanced AI algorithms to recommend books tailored to your interests and reading habits.

Our mission is to create a seamless and intelligent reading experience by connecting users with books they’ll love, across every genre and category, from classics to modern bestsellers.

Our History

What started as a passion project among book lovers and tech enthusiasts has evolved into a powerful recommendation engine and digital library. BookRight began in Almaty, Kazakhstan, and quickly grew thanks to our innovative technology and user-first mindset.

Our Values

  • Curiosity: We believe in the joy of discovery and aim to foster a love for learning and reading.
  • Technology for Good: We use AI to enhance—not replace—the human connection to books and ideas.
  • User Empowerment: We prioritize data privacy and give readers control over their reading journey.
